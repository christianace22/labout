BloxFruit

Blox Fruits is all about gaining experience and leveling up your character to obtain better equipment, weapons, and abilities.


Blox Fruits is a popular Roblox game where you become a pirate and explore a vast world filled with islands, dungeons, and challenging enemies. You can sail the seas, fight bosses, collect powerful fruits that grant you unique abilities, and join or create your own pirate crew to conquer the world. Think of it like a fun, action-packed pirate RPG!

1st Sea 2nd Sea 3rd Sea
First Sea is the starter sea for the game and the location that players spawn in when they start the game. Second Sea is the intermediate sea in the game that players spawn in once they have completed the Military Detective quest. The Third Sea is currently the final sea players will unlock. This sea provides the keystones to many players' PvP builds, such as various powerful fighting styles, swords, and guns. This sea is also the key for Race Awakening.

Descriptions of fruits
Dragon East: A powerful dragon-based fruit with fiery breath and immense destructive power.
Dragon West: Another variation of the Dragon fruit, possibly with different elemental abilities or effects.
Kitsune: A mythical fox fruit granting agility, illusions, and fire-based attacks.
Yeti: A beast-type fruit that enhances strength, ice abilities, and resilience in battle.
Leopard: A Zoan-type fruit that grants incredible speed, agility, and claw-based attacks.
Spirit: A mystical fruit allowing summoning of spirits with different abilities.
Gas: A Logia-type fruit that manipulates toxic gases to suffocate and explode enemies.
Control: Allows the user to manipulate the battlefield, levitate objects, and reshape their surroundings
Venom: A deadly fruit that produces powerful poison-based attacks and transformations.
Shadow: Grants control over darkness, absorbing damage and unleashing shadowy strikes.
Dough: A sticky, elastic fruit enabling powerful melee and ranged attacks with hardened dough.
T-Rex: A massive transformation fruit that grants dinosaur-like strength and durability.
Mammoth: A Zoan-type fruit allowing transformation into a mighty mammoth with devastating attacks.
Blizzard: Controls icy winds and snowstorms to freeze and slow enemies.
Pain: Inflicts severe damage by redirecting and amplifying received pain.
Rumble: Wields electrical powers to shock and paralyze opponents.
